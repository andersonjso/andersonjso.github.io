Anderson Oliveira

OPUS Research Group · PUC - Rio, Brazil ·

Anderson Oliveira is a PhD student in Computer Science at PUC-Rio (Brazil). He received his Master's degree in Informatics at PUC-Rio, advised by Alessandro Garcia, with an emphasis on Software Engineering, working mainly on the identification of architectural problems. He got his Bachelor degree in Computer Science at the Federal University of Alagoas, advised by Marcelo Oliveira, where he carried out scientific initiation research projects, related to computer graphics and data visualisation. He is part of the Opus Research Group, at the Software Engineering Laboratory at PUC-Rio.
